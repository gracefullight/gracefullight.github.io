## Boolean Functions

$$f: \{0,1\}^n \to \{0,1\}$$

| x | $f_0$ | $f_1$ | $f_2$ | $f_3$ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 0 | 0 | 0 | 1 | 1 |
| 1 | 0 | 1 | 0 | 1 |

- $n=1$
- **Constant**: smae output for all inputs ($f_0$ and $f_3$).
- **Balanced**: outputs 0 for exactly half, 1 for the other half ($f_1$ and $f_2$).
- In the worst case, need $2^{n-1}+1$ quries to decide which type $f$ is exponetial in $n$.
