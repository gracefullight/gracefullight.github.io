## Vocabulary & Expressions

| Term/Expression | Definition | Simpler Paraphrase | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |

| coercion | the practice of making someone do something by using force or threats | forceful persuasion | 강제, 강압 |

| diplomatic | relating to the profession, skill or activity of managing relationships between countries | tactful | 외교의, 외교적인 |

| incompetent | not having the necessary skills or ability to do something successfully | unskilled | 무능한, 능력 없는 |
